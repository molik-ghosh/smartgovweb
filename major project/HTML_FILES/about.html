About SmartGovWeb

Your trusted citizen portal for government services, area information, and public utilities.

Our Mission

SmartGovWeb bridges the gap between citizens and government services. Real-time area info, engineer contacts, work status, maps, complaint filing, and profile access all live in one place.

Key Features

Area Directory

Find JE, EE, SE contacts and work status for your locality with interactive maps.

Complaint Portal

File complaints directly linked to your area engineers with photo upload and status tracking.

Admin Panel

Secure data management for authorized administrators with real-time updates.

Our Team

Developed for public service using modern web technologies and Firebase-backed realtime data sync.
